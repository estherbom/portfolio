SAE BOM CHOI

I am a UI/UX designer working with the ALEKS team at McGraw Hill Education. Previously, I led design at DoubleDoor Technologies Inc, a fast paced tech start-up, where I created and managed an end-to-end design process with a user-centered design methodology.


I began my design journey after graduating from college, where my passion for fine arts turned into creating impactful designs for users.


Receiving a bachelor's degree in fine art and having a diverse aspect in design, I am able to bring a unique perspective to the UX design process. When designing products, I understand the importance of identifying the problem and being able to think out of the box to find creative ways of developing a solution that results in a delightful user experience.
